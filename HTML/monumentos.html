Palacio de los Duques de Alba

Palace of the Dukes of Alba

Mandado construir por D. Fernando de Silva y Álvarez de Toledo, y obra del arquitecto Jacques Marquet, traído a España por el duque para edificar el palacio de estilo barroco francés, se construyó donde estaba el antiguo castillo de los Álvarez de Toledo.

Commissioned by Don Fernando de Silva y Álvarez de Toledo and designed by the architect Jacques Marquet brought to Spain by the duke to build the palace in the French Baroque style it was constructed on the site of the former Álvarez de Toledo castle.

Iglesia de Santa María la Mayor

Church of Saint Mary the Great

Fue comenzada en el XIII, sobre una fortaleza del Siglo XII, que donó la Reina Doña Berenguela de Castilla “La Grande”, reina de León, quien lo recibió de su padre Alfonso VIII.

It was begun in the 13th century on the site of a 12th-century fortress, which was donated by Queen Berenguela of Castile, known as “the Great,” Queen of León, who had received it from her father, Alfonso VIII.
